Hi, I'm Junita!

Information System Student · UI/UX Enthusiast · Guitarist

I'm Junita Randa Liling, an Information System student at Universitas Internasional Batam. I move in the world of technology, but I don't live in just one dimension.


I have a strong passion for UI/UX design and front-end development. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics — it's about creating experiences that feel intuitive, meaningful, and human.
